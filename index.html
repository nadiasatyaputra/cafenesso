Welcome to Cafe Nesso.

Every tired student’s cosy coffee escape, Cafe Nesso is conveniently located in the Learning and Teaching Building at Monash University’s Clayton campus. With an environment perfect for well deserved study breaks or catch ups with friends, you’ll find a blend of comfort and excellence in every cup.

  • Cafe Menus

    A la carte breakfast, a burger menu, and international cuisines.

  • Freshly Produced

    Prepared daily by specialty chefs, catering to everyone’s taste.

  • Variety of Coffee

    A selection of freshly roasted coffee, including our unique house blend.
